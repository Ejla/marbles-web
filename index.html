Why Choose Marbles?

All-in-One Convenience

Marbles combines journaling, list-making, and habit tracking into a single, easy-to-use app - eliminating the need for multiple tools. Everything you need for everyday organization is right there, making your life simpler and more streamlined.

Real Privacy

You can control your data with true client-side encryption, ensuring your thoughts and plans stay private and secure. No one else, not even us, can access your information, giving you peace of mind.

Intuitive Design

Marbles is designed with small usability gems like quick list management, adjustable fonts, light and dark mode, keyboard support, making it simple and enjoyable to use every day. No complex setup and workflows, just straightforward online tools.

Designed for Peace of Mind

Beyond just organization, Marbles provides a quiet, clutter-free digital space that helps clear your head and focus on what matters most. It's not just an app - it’s your daily companion for mental clarity and thoughtful living.
